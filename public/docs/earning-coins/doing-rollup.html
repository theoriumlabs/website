Rollup calculation

Recursive rollups are at the heart of the succintness, and it is the most computationally intensive part of operating the chain. When you partake in the rollup process your GPU is utilized to generate proofs of the validity of proofs. This replaces N proofs, each attesting to their correct state update, with a single proof aggregating fees and state updates. E.g. proof1 is for A1 => A2 , proof2 is for A2 => A3 , then a rollup eliminates the need for these and we get a proof3 is for A1 => A3
